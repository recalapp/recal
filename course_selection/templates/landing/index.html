Why ReCal instead of TigerHub?

We're so proud of the University for releasing TigerHub, but we know it's a work in progress. We built a nicer course selection interface from our independent work project, named ReCal — short for Rethinking Calendar. We figured we'd release it while TigerHub is being improved.

ReCal is faster, sleeker, and easier to use.

Coming in Fall 2015:

We'll be launching the full ReCal project. The idea:

Everyone can track deadlines, exam times, and office hours in one shared class calendar, so changes are instant and it's always up to date. No more searching through Blackboard or email for when office hours are this week or when the next homework is due.
